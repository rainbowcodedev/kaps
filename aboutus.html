Who is Kooki Kaps?

Kooki Kaps consists of a couple of textile artists who love to create things! We are located in the Fort Collins area of Northern Colorado.

We make hats for anyone.

But if you are a parent, especially with young children, and you are looking to purchase animal themed fleece hats, then this is for you!

We do custom orders! If you have something specific in mind, just contact us! Feel free to ask about any type of ears you can come up with.
