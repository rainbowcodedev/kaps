Custom Orders

We do custom orders if you have something specific in mind, and you don't see it! Feel free to ask about any type of ears you can come up with, character hats, or whatever other ideas you have! We have made Elmo hats, panda hats, and more!

Hat Sizing Guidelines

  • Extra small is a baby's hat. Brim circumference: 19 inches.
  • Small usually fits ages 7 and under. Brim circumference: 21 inches.
  • Medium will usually fit ages 8 and up and fits most women. Brim circumference: 22.5 inches.
  • Large will fit most adult men. Brim circumference: 23.5 inches.
